Welcome to the **Takwara Technology Mentoring** knowledge base.

**Ancestral Futurism — High-Tech Vernacular Architecture.**

Here we structure the journey of transforming ancestral knowledge of bamboo and vegetable polyurethane into cutting-edge technology, backed by scientific research, laboratory testing (Itecons), and innovation prospects for Amazonian bioeconomy and sustainable construction.

---

## Base Engagement & Instruction (The Bioeconomy Era)

The initial public material is based on the **[Bamboo Bioeconomy Manual](https://takwaratec.github.io/projetos/01_TECNOLOGIA_TAKWARA/TAK_manual-bioeconomia-bambu/)**, introducing students to fundamental concepts before the technical mentoring. Key pillars:

*   **[Phytoremediation and Carbon](https://takwaratec.github.io/projetos/01_TECNOLOGIA_TAKWARA/TAK_manual-fitorremediacao-credito-carbono/)**: How planting regenerates soils and translates into Carbon capture.
*   **[Ecological Sanitation](https://takwaratec.github.io/projetos/02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/TAK_nota-tecnica-saneamento-ecologico/)**: Using bamboo in clean rural infrastructure.
*   **[Poison-Free Treatment](https://takwaratec.github.io/projetos/02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/TAK_tratamento-sem-veneno/)**: Shifting from agrochemicals to natural treatment (The Boron Myth and Ash Milk).
